RomanNepaliAI

Type romanized Nepali below and see it transliterated to Devanagari live, entirely in your browser (no server, no data leaves your machine). This is the same engine used by the --transliterate flag in the CLI. English translation below is optional and calls a small backend. Mic (🎤) and speaker (🔊) buttons use your browser's built-in speech support when available — support and Nepali voice quality vary by browser/device.
